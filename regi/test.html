View Hike Rating Key
Example: B3B
5-9 miles (B)
moderately paced (3)
on strenuous terrain (B)
First letters indicate mileage:
AA = 13 mi and over
A = 9 to 13 mi
B = 5 to 9 mi
C = under 5 mi
Middle number indicates pace across "average" terrain:
1= very fast (2.5 mph+)
2= fast (2 to 2.5 mph)
3= moderate (1.5 to 2 mph)
4= leisurely (0.5 to 1.5 mph)
End letters indicate terrain:
A= very strenuous
A= very strenuous
C= average
D= easy
Notes:
Terrain ratings may not match Connecticut or Southeastern Massachusetts values.
Backpacks rate the most difficult day of hiking planned.
Some ratings may have an additional two letters at the end, these indicate a terrain level between the values.
Actual trip pace may differ from the pace number if terrain is not "average".
